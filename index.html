About me

Hi, I'm Kai. I am a 3rd year Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo. I am interested in algorithms, machine learning, and competitive programming.

I am currently a Research Intern at Uber ATG working on self-driving car research and development. I have previously worked at MemSQL as a Software Engineering Intern on the Query Optimization team, speeding up the distributed optimizer by 16-40x and developing a heuristic search algorithm to find good query plans for 25-40 joins in just 2-5 seconds. I have also worked at BlackBerry as an Android Software Development Student, improving the BlackBerry Hub+ Inbox, Calendar, Contacts, Tasks, and Notes apps.

I have a competitive math and programming background. I achieved a Silver Medal at the International Mathematical Olympiad in 2016, was on the 4th Place Team in the ACM-ICPC East Central North America Regional Contest in 2018, and received Honorable Mentions in the William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition in 2018 and 2019.

Chat for Class

A messaging app for schools that allows students and teachers to branch new conversations from the current one, for a more organized and efficient method of communication.

Route Planner

Finds the shortest route visiting a list of up to 10 places, specified by exact address or a place name. Used Bitmask Dynamic Programming, Breadth First Search, Google Maps Places API, Directions API, and Distance Matrix API.

Built with Java, XML, SQLite, and Android Studio.

Built with C++.
